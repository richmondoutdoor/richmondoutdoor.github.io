11 - 13 May 2018

Join us!

The Richmond Outdoor is a 3-day outdoor family weekend, comprising of a 3-day mountain bike stage race for two-member teams, and a 5km, 10km and 21km run or walk through the majestic Upper Karoo. It is held in and around South Africa’s only official Booktown, Richmond, located on the N1 in the Northern Cape.

The race is characterised by awe-inspiring landscapes and water points that will have you turning around for more.

Make sure to drop by the Crow’s Nest, Vegkop, Modern Art Projects and the historic Church and Museum while you’re in town.
